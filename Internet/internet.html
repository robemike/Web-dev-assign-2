The Internet

What is the internet?

The Internet is a world-wide system of interconnected computers cooperating to exchange data using a common software standard (protocols) through telephone lines and satellite links. It started in 1969 as a US military experiment (ARPANET).

The following are aspects of the internet:

  • → HTTP (Hypertext Transfer Protocol)
  • → HTTPS (Hypertext Transfer Protocol Secure)
